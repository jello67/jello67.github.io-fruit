Fresh Berry Smoothie Bowl

Start your day with this colorful and nutritious smoothie bowl packed with antioxidants!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Toppings of choice

Instructions:

  • Blend frozen banana, berries, yogurt, and honey until smooth and thick
  • Pour into a bowl
  • Add your favorite toppings in colorful rows
  • Enjoy immediately for the best texture!

Tropical Mango Salsa

A fresh and colorful salsa perfect for chips

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 serrano, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • seasonings of choice

Instructions:

  • Combine diced mango, red onion, and jalapeño in a bowl
  • Add cilantro and lime juice
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • Let sit for 15 minutes for flavors to blend
  • Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping

Classic Peach Cobbler

A warm, comforting dessert to use up fresh peaches

Ingredients:

  • 6 sliced peaches
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Citrus Salad Dressing

A refreshing salad dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup grapefruit juice
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Instructions:

  • Whisk together all citrus juices in a bowl
  • Add honey and Dijon mustard
  • Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking
